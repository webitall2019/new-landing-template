Why Choose Company

Company is designed to offer the best support to moms to be, and moms going through the postpartum experience. The social platform offers advice, a chance to connect and socialise, and most importantly the opportunity for both doulas and moms to find and give valuable care.

At Company your experience is valid and all concerns are relevant. The benefit of a community is that as the network grows there will always be someone who is listening and may have
information and stories to share that help you gain the confidence to seek support.

What is the typical response time for a support question?
Since you report us a support question we try to make our best to find out what is going on, depending on the case it could take more or les time, however a standard time could be minutes or hours.
